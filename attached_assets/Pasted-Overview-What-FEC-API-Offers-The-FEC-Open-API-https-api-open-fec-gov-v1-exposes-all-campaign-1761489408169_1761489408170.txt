Overview ‚Äî What FEC API Offers

The FEC Open API (https://api.open.fec.gov/v1/) exposes all campaign finance data filed with the U.S. Federal Election Commission ‚Äî covering donors, PACs, candidates, committees, expenditures, and election filings.

You can use it to track:

Which corporations or PACs gave money

Who the recipients were (candidates or committees)

When and how much was given

Which party or office (Senate, House, President) the recipients belong to

What the money was used for (disbursement purpose)

And even how much each PAC has on hand or spends in each election cycle

‚öôÔ∏è Main Endpoints You‚Äôll Use
Endpoint	What it returns	Useful for your app
/committees/	All registered political committees (PACs, candidate committees, party committees)	Find corporate PACs linked to companies
/disbursements/	Outgoing payments made by committees	Show how a PAC spends money (donations, admin, advertising, etc.)
/schedule_a/	Contributions received by a committee	Show who funds a PAC
/candidates/	Candidate information (party, state, office)	Link recipients to their political party
/totals/	Financial summary for a candidate or committee	Get total receipts/disbursements for a cycle
/committee/{committee_id}/reports/	Official filed financial reports	Confirm totals per reporting period
/elections/	Election-level aggregates	Optional (for context or filters)
